Welcome to Cakesboutique, nestled in the heart of Cheruthuruthy, Thrissur District. With a legacy spanning three years, we take pride in being purveyors of exquisite baked creations. From delectable pizzas to luscious puddings and the authentic taste of kunaffa, our kitchen is a haven for culinary delights.

Why Choose Cakesboutique?

Over the past three years, we've embarked on a delightful journey of baking perfection. With a commitment to quality and a sprinkle of creativity, Cakesboutique has become synonymous with exceptional taste and culinary craftsmanship.

Learn More

Our Signature Touch

What sets us apart is not just the ingredients we use, but the passion and dedication that goes into every creation. Our skilled bakers infuse love into every batter, and each cake is a testament to our commitment to delivering an unparalleled culinary experience.

Our Milestones

Having served over 800 clients, we take pride in having delivered more than 1000 baked items to delighted patrons. It's not just about the numbers; it's about the smiles we've earned and the moments we've sweetened with our confections.

Join Us in the Celebration

At Cakesboutique, we invite you to join us in the celebration of flavors, textures, and moments. Whether it's a special occasion or a simple craving, our diverse range of baked goods is designed to elevate your experience and leave a lasting impression.
